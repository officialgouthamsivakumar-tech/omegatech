> **1. ABOUT OMEGA TECHNICAL SOLUTIONS LTD**

Omega Technical Solutions Ltd is a fully registered and operational
heavy equipment company headquartered in Nairobi, Kenya. With more than
10 years of active trading, rental, and maintenance operations across
East Africa, Omega Technical Solutions Ltd has built a respected
reputation as a reliable partner to construction companies, quarry
operators, infrastructure developers, and government contractors across
the region.

The company was founded on the principle of delivering high-quality
equipment solutions with genuine after-sales commitment --- a philosophy
that has driven our growth from a local equipment trader to a
multi-country distributor with an official Annual Maintenance Contract
(AMC) certification and an established customer base spanning Kenya,
Tanzania, Uganda, Ethiopia, Rwanda, and South Sudan.

Today, Omega Technical Solutions Ltd is seeking to expand its dealership
portfolio to include a leading crusher brand --- bringing
manufacturer-grade crushing equipment, genuine spare parts, and
certified after-sales support directly to East Africa\'s rapidly growing
construction and quarrying sectors.

> **9. EAST AFRICA MARKET OPPORTUNITY --- CRUSHER SEGMENT**

East Africa represents one of the fastest-growing construction equipment
markets on the continent. The region\'s infrastructure development
agenda --- including major road networks, housing programs, dam
construction, and urban expansion --- has created strong and sustained
demand for aggregate crushing equipment across all countries in which
Omega Technical Solutions Ltd operates.

**Key Market Drivers**

- Kenya\'s ongoing road construction and rehabilitation programs require
  consistent aggregate supply --- driving crusher demand across the
  country.

- East Africa\'s rapidly growing urbanization is fueling housing
  construction, creating sustained demand for gravel and crushed stone.

- Major infrastructure projects including dams, airports, ports, and
  industrial zones in Ethiopia, Tanzania, and Uganda require large-scale
  crushing operations.

- The quarrying sector in Kenya and Tanzania is expanding rapidly, with
  increasing numbers of quarry operators seeking modern, efficient
  crushing solutions.

- Growing awareness of equipment quality means buyers are increasingly
  seeking branded, supported crushing equipment over unbranded
  alternatives.

**Omega Technical Solutions Ltd\'s Market Advantage**

  one.

- Our rental fleet enables live product demonstrations --- customers can
  see the equipment perform before purchasing.

- Our trade-in capability removes the most common barrier to new
  equipment purchase.

- Our AMC certification means customers receive full lifecycle support
  --- increasing confidence in purchasing a new brand.
